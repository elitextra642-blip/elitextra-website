About the company

Built for buyers who need Nigerian supply they can trust.

Elitextra is an agricultural export partner based around practical sourcing work: finding the right product, preparing it properly, and keeping buyers informed before shipments leave Nigeria.

See sourcing model → View products

Origin story

A better bridge between farms, processors, and buyers.

Nigerian staples and specialty ingredients travel far, but too often they move through fragmented channels. Buyers want clearer communication, stronger packaging discipline, and product consistency they can plan around.

Elitextra was shaped around that need. The company works with farming and processing partners across key Nigerian supply regions, then organizes the practical export steps buyers care about: product matching, grading, packaging, documentation, and follow-through.
