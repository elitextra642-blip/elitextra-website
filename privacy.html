Privacy

How buyer information is handled.

This page explains how Elitextra handles inquiry details submitted through this website. It is a practical publish-ready policy and should be reviewed by counsel before high-volume commercial launch.

Clear use, no noise.

When you contact Elitextra, we may collect your name, company, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, destination country, product interests, quantity range, and message details.

We use this information to respond to inquiries, prepare product or pricing discussions, coordinate samples, and improve buyer communication.

We do not sell buyer inquiry information. We may share relevant details with logistics, quality, or fulfilment partners only when needed to respond to an inquiry or prepare an order.

To request correction or removal of your inquiry details, contact elitextra642@gmail.com.
